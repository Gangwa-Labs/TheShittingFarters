Atmosleep’s MVP1 consists of a pillow with a built-in speaker. The product will allow users to connect to a Bluetooth speaker and play materials such as meditation, podcasts, and lectures as they sleep. The comfort pillow will be made from a cooling stuffing such as memory foam with cooling gel. The speaker will be slim and not noticeable by the touch of the pillow.

Atmosleep’s MVP2 is an app that customizes the learning process while sleeping. The app allows users to input their own study material and have it read to them in their sleep. The app will also have a sleep quality tracker that will ensure that information is relayed only during certain times of one’s REM cycle. MVP3 will potentially combine MVP1 and MVP2.
