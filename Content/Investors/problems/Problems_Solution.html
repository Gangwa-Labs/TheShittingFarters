Our Problem & Solution

In a world increasingly strapped for time, Atmosleep aims to remove the decision between sacrificing work or sacrificing sleep. Atmosleep hopes to create a product that relieves stress and promotes wellness.

Target
Market

Atmosleep operates within the sleep wellness and learning industry. Atmosleep is addressing the customer job to be done of maximizing sleep and learning. Atmosleep’s product combines sleep and learning, situating it at the intersection of the sleep and learning industries. As the sleep learning industry is still relatively new, Atmosleep resides within a blue ocean because no other products directly address the job to be done of maximizing sleep and learning.
